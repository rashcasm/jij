Occult

Crushed and curled up, she's lying in a corner

Hands full of dreams, maybe she's dying in that corner.

The voice, she got, isn't loud anymore.
She's trying to pick a broken soul, that shattered before.

The best of her is already wasted.
She's the 'pretty witch', who is always hated.

Her body is full of scars, more deeper than her past.
Luckily, the poisonous breath inside her didn't last.

~Harshita

We are just kids.

We are just kids in war.

With dolls in hands, standing so far.
Far away from the city of chaos.

With bleak sights after the family loss.
No place left for us to grow.
Not even someone to share our sorrow.
But we don't wanna die
Or maybe we will... tonight.

We are just kids in war.
With tears in eyes, standing so far.
We heard them screaming,
Wondered if it was just the beginning.
We have seen bleeding bodies piled up.
Kept in the coffin of hatred, all lined up.
But we don't wanna die
Or maybe we will... tonight.

We are just kids in war.
With shattered hearts, standing so far.
We don't understand your boundaries or religion.
But now this city of corpse is a prison.
We don't know if they are terrorists or politicians.
But for them, we are just an exception.
We are all alone and our tears have already dried.
Who cares for infants and for the people who have died?

~Harshita

God exists?

You say God exists

So tell me please
What kind of God would let this happen?
What kind of God will watch a soul burn in heaven?
You say that he hear, help and bless
But what kind of God would see this mess?
God has allowed too much but he can't kill my empathy.
And if God exists then why he never show his sympathy.
And if there is a God then tell him to beg for my forgiveness.
I saw people screaming his name, I call it their foolishness.
I will go and tell them that your God is dead,
And go worship sin as it's been said.
So if god exists then why is he blind
And is he really a god or just a Satan of other kind?

~Harshita

Nirvana

If you leave me,

My rotten body on ground.
Maybe flowers will grow
From my biggest wound.
They will grow very bright,
Deep from my blank mind.
It might be a wonderful sight
For the people of your kind.
You might see roots all around my heart
Only if you dare to scratch all the upper parts.
You might wonder how my limbs look so green
Because they forgot how to scream,
When each piece was torn perfectly with beak.
Veins announced their end, now no one to seek.

But people say if you leave my rotten body on ground
It will be eaten by a man or hound.
They say the second one hurts less
Even though both of them looks for some flesh.

~Harshita
